VParser

  • Download Latest

A powerful Android library to sniff the real video URL from video sites.

Introduction

  • Are you looking for a way to parse real video urls from some video sites?
  • Do you think it's awful to waste time for parsing so many video sites to get the real video urls?

OK, VParser is really a good choice to finish above bad conditions.

HOW TO USE

VParser is powerful and easy to use!

Video video = new VParser(context).parse(url);
String videoTitle = video.title;
String videoUri = video.videoUri;
